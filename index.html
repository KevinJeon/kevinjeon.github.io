Hyeonchang Jeon

I'm a Ph.D student from GIST AI, advised by Kyung-Joong Kim

My research goal is to understand how decision-making models interpret and use information about other agents across diverse multi-agent domains. I study how an agent can reason about others' behavior and incorporate that understanding into its own decisions.

Recently, I have been focusing on this problem in the autonomous driving domain. Previously, I worked on multi-agent reinforcement learning in games.

Beyond multi-agent decision-making for autonomous driving, I'm also interested in trajectory generation and open-endedness for autonomous driving. If you'd like to discuss or collaborate on any of these topics, feel free to reach out!
